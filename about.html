About JERWIN

JERWIN is an Austin, Texas–based pop and hip-hop artist known for high-energy, humorous, and feel-good songs rooted in everyday life. Blending comedy music, motivational hip-hop, and theatrical pop, his tracks turn relatable moments into catchy, replay-worthy anthems.

From upbeat songs like Go Bananas to self-deprecating golf humor in I Suck At Golf, JERWIN leans into playful storytelling, punchy hooks, and crowd-ready energy.

Whether you’re searching for funny songs, clean family-friendly tracks, or uplifting pop and hip-hop, JERWIN creates music designed to entertain, motivate, and make people smile.
